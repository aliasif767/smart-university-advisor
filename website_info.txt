# Smart Advisor — Student Portal Knowledge Base
# This file is used by the AI chatbot to answer student questions accurately.

## ABOUT THE SYSTEM

Smart Advisor is a university student management portal. It connects students, teachers, advisors, and the Head of Program (HOP) in a structured workflow. Students can submit queries, book appointments with their advisor, view attendance and marks, and get help from an AI assistant.

There are four roles in the system:
- Student: submits queries, books appointments, views academic records
- Teacher: marks attendance, uploads marks, handles exam and attendance queries
- Advisor: reviews leave and academic queries, manages appointments, monitors student progress
- HOP (Head of Program): gives final approval on advisor-forwarded queries, manages announcements


## DASHBOARD OVERVIEW

After logging in, students see the main dashboard with:
- Pending Requests count
- Approved queries count
- Active Courses count
- Recent applications list
- Announcements from HOP
- Quick action buttons: Submit New Query, Book Appointment, AI Assistant

Sidebar navigation includes:
- Dashboard (home)
- Submit Query (form to submit a new request)
- My Attendance (personal attendance records per course)
- My Marks (exam and assignment marks per course)
- My Queries (all submitted queries with status and approval pipeline)
- My Appointments (all booked appointments with advisor response)


## HOW TO SUBMIT A QUERY

Students can submit four types of queries through the portal. Each type goes to the correct person for review.

### Step-by-step:
1. Click "Submit Query" in the sidebar or "Submit New Query" button on the dashboard
2. Choose a category tab: Academic, Exam, Leave, or Other
3. Select the specific query type from the dropdown
4. Fill in the required fields (they change depending on the type)
5. Add a description explaining your situation
6. Optionally attach a supporting document (PDF, JPG, PNG, DOC — max 5MB)
7. Click Submit

### Query Categories and Types:

**Academic Queries** (go to Advisor → HOP):
- Course Add/Drop: request to add or drop a course this semester
- Course Freeze: request to freeze a course (pause enrollment)
  - Required fields: Course Name, Course Code (optional), Description

**Exam Queries** (go to Teacher only):
- Mid Retake: request to retake a midterm exam
- Final Retake: request to retake a final exam
- Update Marks: request correction of recorded marks
  - Required fields: Course Name, Course Code (optional), Exam Type (mid/final/quiz), Current Marks (optional), Description

**Leave Queries** (go to Advisor → HOP):
- Sick Leave: medical absence
- Marriage Leave: leave for marriage ceremony
- Urgent Leave: emergency or urgent personal matter
  - Required fields: Leave Type, Start Date, End Date, Description

**Other Queries** (go to Teacher only):
- Attendance Issue: dispute or correction of attendance record
  - Required fields: Course Name, Course Code, Description
- Timetable Issue: problems with class schedule
  - Required fields: Description only

### Priority Levels:
Queries can be set to low, medium, or high priority. Default is medium.

### Supported file types for attachments:
PDF, DOC, DOCX, JPG, JPEG, PNG — maximum 5MB per file.


## APPROVAL PIPELINE

Different query types follow different approval paths.

### Exam and Attendance/Other Queries — Teacher Only:
The assigned teacher reviews and approves or rejects the query. No advisor or HOP involvement.
- If teacher approves → query is approved (finalStatus: approved)
- If teacher rejects → query is rejected (finalStatus: rejected) with a rejection reason

### Leave and Academic Queries — Advisor then HOP:
1. Advisor reviews first: can approve (forward to HOP) or reject
2. If advisor approves → HOP reviews next: can approve or reject
3. If HOP approves → query is fully approved (finalStatus: approved)
4. If advisor or HOP rejects → query is rejected (finalStatus: rejected) with a rejection reason

### Status values:
- pending: waiting for review
- approved: fully approved by all required parties
- rejected: rejected by teacher, advisor, or HOP

Students can see the current approval stage and any rejection reasons on the My Queries page.


## HOW TO VIEW MY QUERIES

1. Click "My Queries" in the sidebar
2. All submitted queries are shown as cards
3. Filter by: All, Pending, Approved, Rejected
4. Each card shows:
   - Query type and category
   - Priority badge
   - Description
   - Course name (if applicable)
   - Leave dates (if applicable)
   - Final status badge (PENDING / APPROVED / REJECTED)
   - Approval Pipeline showing who reviewed it and the outcome
   - Rejection reason (shown in red only when rejected)
   - Submission date and time
5. Pending queries can be deleted using the Delete button on the card


## HOW TO BOOK AN APPOINTMENT

Students can book an appointment with their advisor through the portal.

### Method 1 — Dashboard button:
1. Click "Book Appointment" on the main dashboard quick actions
2. A form appears asking for:
   - Appointment Type (Academic / Course / Career / Personal / Counseling)
   - Preferred Date
   - Preferred Time
   - Reason for the appointment
3. Click "Book Appointment" to submit

### Method 2 — My Appointments page:
1. Click "My Appointments" in the sidebar
2. Click "Book New Appointment" button at the top
3. Fill in the same form fields
4. Submit

### Method 3 — AI Assistant:
Say "Book an appointment for me" to the AI chatbot and it will collect all details through conversation and submit it automatically.

### Appointment Types:
- academic: academic planning and course guidance
- course: specific course-related advice
- career: career planning and advice
- personal: personal matters
- counseling: counseling and wellbeing support

### After booking:
The appointment will appear on your My Appointments page with status "Pending". Your advisor will review it and either confirm or cancel it. You will see their response, confirmed date and time, and any comments directly on the My Appointments page.

### Appointment Statuses:
- Pending: waiting for advisor to respond
- Confirmed: advisor has accepted and set a date/time
- Rescheduled: advisor has set a different date/time
- Cancelled: advisor has cancelled (cancellation reason shown)
- Completed: the meeting has taken place


## HOW TO VIEW MY APPOINTMENTS

1. Click "My Appointments" in the sidebar
2. All appointments are shown as cards
3. Summary counts at the top show Total, Confirmed, and Pending counts
4. Each card shows:
   - Appointment type and reason
   - Requested date and time
   - Status badge (color coded)
   - Advisor Response section (appears once advisor acts):
     - For Confirmed: a green box showing the confirmed meeting date and time
     - For Cancelled: a red box showing the cancellation reason
     - For Rescheduled: green box with the new date and time
5. Pending appointments can be cancelled using the Cancel button


## HOW TO VIEW ATTENDANCE

1. Click "My Attendance" in the sidebar
2. All attendance records are shown, sorted by most recent date
3. Each record shows: date, course name, course code, status (present/absent/late), and remarks
4. Attendance is recorded by your assigned teacher
5. If you believe an attendance record is wrong, submit an Attendance Issue query


## HOW TO VIEW MARKS

1. Click "My Marks" in the sidebar
2. All exam and assignment marks are shown
3. Each record shows: course name, course code, exam type, exam date, marks obtained, total marks, percentage, and remarks
4. Marks are uploaded by your assigned teacher
5. If you believe marks are recorded incorrectly, submit an Update Marks query (exam category)


## AI ASSISTANT

The AI assistant can help students in two ways:

### Guidance Mode (asking "how"):
Ask questions like:
- "How do I book an appointment?"
- "What is the leave application process?"
- "How to request a course drop?"
- "What documents do I need for sick leave?"
- "What happens after I submit a query?"
The AI will explain the process step by step.

### Action Mode (saying "do it for me"):
Say things like:
- "Book an appointment for me"
- "Apply for sick leave"
- "Drop a course for me"
- "Submit an attendance issue"
- "Check my query status"
- "Check my appointments"
The AI will ask you each required detail one by one through conversation, show you a summary, and submit the request after you confirm.

The AI assistant can:
- Book advisor appointments
- Submit leave queries (sick, marriage, urgent)
- Submit academic queries (add/drop, freeze)
- Submit exam queries (retake, marks update)
- Submit other queries (attendance, timetable)
- Check the status of your submitted queries
- Check your booked appointments


## QUERY STATUS TRACKING

After submitting a query, students can track it in My Queries:

- The overall status shows at the top right of each card as PENDING, APPROVED, or REJECTED
- The Approval Pipeline below shows each reviewer's status individually
- For exam and attendance queries: only the Teacher step is shown
- For leave and academic queries: Advisor and HOP steps are shown in order
- If a query is rejected at any stage, the rejection reason from that reviewer is shown in a red box below the pipeline
- Queries with pending status can be deleted; approved or rejected queries cannot be deleted


## ANNOUNCEMENTS

HOP (Head of Program) posts announcements that are visible to all students on the main dashboard. These may include exam schedules, university events, urgent notices, or general information.


## ROLES AND RESPONSIBILITIES

### Student
- Submits queries and appointments
- Views own attendance, marks, queries, appointments
- Can delete pending queries
- Can cancel pending appointments

### Teacher
- Handles exam queries (retake requests, marks updates)
- Handles other queries (attendance issues, timetable issues)
- Marks student attendance (present/absent/late per course)
- Uploads exam and assignment marks
- Can approve or reject teacher-handled queries with comments

### Advisor
- Handles leave queries (sick, marriage, urgent) and academic queries (add/drop, freeze)
- Can approve (forward to HOP) or reject those queries with comments
- Confirms or cancels student appointment requests
- Monitors student attendance and marks
- Registers new students and assigns teachers

### HOP (Head of Program)
- Gives final approval on advisor-forwarded leave and academic queries
- Can approve or reject with comments
- Views all confirmed appointments
- Posts announcements to all users


## FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

### Q: How long does approval take?
A: There is no fixed deadline. The query moves through the pipeline as each reviewer acts. You can track the current stage in My Queries at any time.

### Q: Can I submit multiple queries at the same time?
A: Yes. You can submit as many queries as needed. Each is tracked independently.

### Q: Can I cancel a query after submitting?
A: Yes, but only if the query is still pending. Once a query has been approved or rejected, it cannot be deleted.

### Q: What happens if my query is rejected?
A: The query is marked as Rejected and the reviewer's reason is shown in red on your My Queries page. You can submit a new query if the situation changes.

### Q: Who reviews my leave query?
A: First your Advisor reviews it. If the Advisor approves, it is forwarded to the HOP for final approval. If either rejects it, you will see their rejection reason.

### Q: Who reviews my exam query (retake or marks update)?
A: Your assigned Teacher reviews it. The Advisor and HOP are not involved in exam queries.

### Q: Who reviews my attendance issue?
A: Your assigned Teacher reviews it. The Advisor and HOP are not involved.

### Q: What happens after my appointment is confirmed?
A: You will see the confirmed date and time in a green box on your My Appointments page. Attend the meeting at that scheduled time.

### Q: Can I cancel an appointment?
A: Yes, but only if the appointment is still in Pending status. Click the Cancel button on the appointment card in My Appointments.

### Q: What if I need to check my attendance percentage?
A: Go to My Attendance in the sidebar. All records are listed with present, absent, and late statuses per course. If you believe a record is wrong, submit an Attendance Issue query.

### Q: How do I report a wrong mark?
A: Submit an Update Marks query under the Exam category. Select the course, exam type, enter your current marks, and describe the issue. Your teacher will review it.

### Q: Can the AI assistant actually submit things for me?
A: Yes. Tell the AI "Book an appointment for me" or "Apply for sick leave" and it will collect all required details through conversation and submit the request after you confirm the summary. You will see the result immediately in My Appointments or My Queries.

### Q: What file types can I attach to a query?
A: PDF, DOC, DOCX, JPG, JPEG, and PNG files are accepted. Maximum size is 5MB per file.

### Q: What is the batch and semester used for?
A: Your batch (e.g. 2022) and semester are assigned when your advisor registers you. They are used to group students and link you to the correct advisor and teacher.
